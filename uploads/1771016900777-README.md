- **Engagement Rate:** Likes + comments per view

- **Total Views:** Growth trajectory

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use this system with other AI tools?
**A:** Yes! All prompts work with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI assistant. Just copy-paste and adjust as needed.

### Q: Do I need to be a psychology expert?
**A:** No, but you should verify scientific accuracy. The prompts guide AI to provide research-backed content, but always fact-check.

### Q: How long until I see results?
**A:** Consistent quality content takes 3-6 months to gain traction. Focus on creating 20-30 videos before expecting significant growth.

### Q: Can I adapt this for other niches?
**A:** The structure is adaptable, but the specific prompts are optimized for behavioral psychology and habit content.

### Q: What if I can't afford outsourcing?
**A:** Use AI tools for everything except final quality control. Budget option is ~$50-100 per video using AI for most tasks.

### Q: How do I maintain consistency across outsourced work?
**A:** Use the checklists religiously. Every deliverable should pass the relevant checklist before approval.
